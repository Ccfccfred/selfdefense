Basic Stance

  • start with your least dominate foot infront (around a normal step distance)
  • keep your hands close to your face but not directly infront of it
  • keep your weight balanced and hips towards the directtion your facing

    • next move?



Knee Strike

  • start in basic stance
  • use your arms to grab your target (if there isn't one use your hands as targets)
  • raise your knee while pushing your hips forward for extra strength
  • for extra strength you can pull your hands in while pushing forward with your knee
    • next move?


Front Kick

  • start in the basic stance
  • bring your back knee up similar to a knee strike
  • extend your leg straight and hit the target with the ball of your foot
  • as you extend push your hips forward to increase power
  • put it all together and make it faster
    • next move?


Round Kick

  • start in the basic stance
  • twist your front foot sideways (this will help open up your hips)
  • as you turn your front foot behind you, raise your back leg forward untill your knee and head is aligned
  • extend your leg and hit the target with your instep(top of the foot)
  • once you have these steps pick up the speed and make it one smooth turn
