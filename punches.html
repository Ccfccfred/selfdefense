Basic Stance

  • start with your least dominate foot infront (around a normal step distance)
  • keep your hands close to your face but not directly infront of it
  • keep your weight balanced and hips towards the directtion your facing

    • next move?

Jab & Cross

  • start in basic stance
  • twist your hips slightly towards your front leg
  • while twisting extend the same side arm and twist your wrist so your palm is facing the floor
  • bring the extended arm back
  • twist your hips to bring the other hip infront
  • extend the other arm straight with the same turning motion to bring yout palm down
  • once you have the hang of this try piecing it together

Back Fist

  • start in the basic stance
  • point your eldbow towards your target
  • shift your weight into the front legn while leaning forawrd
  • exend your arm hitting your target with the back of your fist
